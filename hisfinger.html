His Finger

His Finger is a collection of Bible verses inspired by the Word of God.
I created these works to share the message of Jesus with my dear friend, Zhen Zhen, during her journey with terminal stomach cancer. The verses are placed over photographs taken by her husband, photographer Huang Jian. His images reflect the quiet beauty of the horse ranch where he works, sunrise and sunset, and the passing of the seasons.
